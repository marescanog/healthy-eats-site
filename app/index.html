Here’s how we got started…

For Lola Lily’s family, holidays and birthdays translate to delicious, healthy dishes made with love. “Food is not just to fill our bellies but also to sustain our bodies and elevate our well-being. Delicious, sustaining, nourishing and wholesome describes my dishes” is Lola Lily’s mantra. With no formal culinary training, Lola Lily learned how to cook in the ancestral family kitchen, starting out as a kitchen helper at the tender age of three.

Other children her age would be preoccupied by toys, but not Lola Lily. She was fascinated by how food was prepared, from their backyard garden to the dining table. With the family matriarchs as her mentors, she learned that simple dishes can be elevated to gourmet meals, with excellence in quality and freshness of the ingredients being emphasized.

Lola Lily’s passion for food never wavered. Her love for her family and her zeal to create delicious healthy meals merged to come up with a business to provide healthy food straight to the family dining table. Thus healthy food delivery was born.
